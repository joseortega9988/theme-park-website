Rides and Experiences

DinoLand is a fun place for everyone! Spend the day riding our dinosaur themed rides and attractions or enjoy one of many day and evening dinosaur themed shows.

Available for adults and children. Book your experience now!

Our Featured Rides

The Tyranno

Dinoland's oldest and most iconic roller coaster ride. Offers a two minute ride reaching speeds of 40 m.p.h. This ride is not recommended for those who fear heights and fast speeds. Remain seated at all times and enjoy the view.

Height Requirment: 50" minimum

Age Requirment: 10 years or older

The Velocir

Enjoy this swinging ride. Go round and round. The faster the Velocir goes, the more ride tilts offering you a soaring experience.

Height Requirment: 40" minimum OR be accompanied by an adult.

Age Requirment: 10 years or older

Infants are not permitted.

Spinosaurus Speed

Strap-in and brace yourself for this ride. This ride has no mercy for those who fear. The Spinosaurus Speed is our fastest ride reaching speeds of 60 m.p.h. Loop after loop inside and out.

Height Requirment: 40" minimum

Age Requirment: 10 years or older
