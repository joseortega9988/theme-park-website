Rides and Experiences

DinoLand is a fun place for everyone! Spend the day riding our dinosaur themed rides and attractions or enjoy one of many day and evening dinosaur themed shows.

Available for adults and children. Book your experience now!

Our Featured Rides

The Tyranno

Dinoland's oldest and most iconic roller coaster ride. Offers a two minute ride reaching speeds of 40 m.p.h.
This ride is not recommended for those who fear heights and fast speeds.
Remain seated at all times and enjoy the view.

Height Requirment: 50" minimum

Age Requirment: 10 years or older

The Velocir

Enjoy this swinging ride. Go round and round. The faster the Velocir goes, the more ride tilts
offering you a soaring experience.

Height Requirment: 40" minimum OR be accompanied by an adult.

Age Requirment: 10 years or older. Infants are not permitted.

Spinosaurus Speed

Strap-in and brace yourself for this ride. This ride has no mercy for those who fear.
The Spinosaurus Speed is our fastest ride reaching speeds of 60 m.p.h. Loop after loop inside and out.

Height Requirment: 40" minimum

Age Requirment: 10 years or older

Shows and Events

Jurassic Quest

The Jurassic Quest, is a show for all ages. A 45-minute animated show based on the movie Jurassic Park.

Show exhibits every Monday, Thursday and Sunday at 7PM.

Dino Dance Show

The Dino Dance show is for the younger crowds. Bring your kids and have some fun featuring all of the latests dino dances.

Restaurants and Dining

Jungle Cafe

Enjoy our Jungle Cafe. This jungle themed cafe serves food of all types for all appetites.

Open every day from 8:00AM to 10:00PM.

The T-Rex BBQ Pit, offers mouth watering smoked brisket, the finest rotisserie chicken and pork platters as well as your most desired comfort foods.

Open every day from 8:00AM to 10:00PM.

McDondalds

Burgers, fries and more. Enjoy some food in between rides for a quick grab and go or sit in our cafe while you rest from the thrills of the park.

Open everyday from 7:00AM to midnight.
